Introduction

Welcome to Digital Chess Clock! Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we handle and protect your data when using our app.

Data Collection

1. Advertising

We use Appodeal to handle advertisements within the app. They may collect limited information to serve contextual or personalized ads:

  • Device Information: Device model, OS, unique ID, IP address.
  • Location: Approximate (country/city), based on IP address.

Read the Appodeal Privacy Policy for more details.

2. User Data Storage

  • No user data is collected or stored externally.
  • All data such as timer settings and saved matches are stored locally on your device using SQLite.

Third-Party Services

We integrate only Appodeal for ads. All data usage complies with their privacy standards.

Data Security

Digital Chess Clock does not use cloud servers or external APIs. Your app data stays private and secured on your device.

Consent

By using the app, you agree to this privacy policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use.
